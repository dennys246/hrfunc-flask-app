About HRfunc

HRfunc was developed at Washinton University in St. Louis in the Laboratory for Child Brain Development by Denny Schaedig. Denny is a neuroimaging engineer at Washington University and holds a B.S. in Neuroscientist and a M.S. in AI and machine learning. He has a passion for building tools at the intersection of brain science and machine learning.
